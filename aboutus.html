About Us

Info about Scoop's

It has been said that Scoop's Ice Creamery started out as a small ice cream shack with origins traced to a small town in the Midwestern United States region. Nobody knows the exact location or name of the town, but it is suspected to be somewhere in either Michigan, Indiana, or Ohio.

Rumored original location of Scoop's

It wasn't until 1985 when the original owners of Scoop's were rumored to have been approached by a wealthy businessman who offered to take the ice cream shop off their hands for a grand total of $100,000.00. Ever since then, Scoop's has been slowly working its way from an unheard of ice cream shop, to the beloved local business it is today!
